How long does deployment typically take?

Initial deployment typically takes 3-6 months, depending on scope and institutional readiness. Phased rollouts are available.

Yes, our platform includes APIs and connectors for major HRIS, financial, and case management systems. Custom integrations are supported.

What training and support do you provide?

We provide comprehensive training for administrators and users, plus ongoing technical support and system optimization services.

Is the platform available on-premise?

Yes, PSHub can be deployed on-premise, in government clouds, or in private cloud environments based on your requirements.
