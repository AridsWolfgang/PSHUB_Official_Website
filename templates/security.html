FAQs

Security Questions

Do you offer a bug bounty program?

Yes, we maintain a private bug bounty program through HackerOne. Security researchers can request access by contacting security@pshub.io.

Can we perform our own security assessment?

Yes, we support customer-led security assessments with reasonable notice. Contact your account representative to schedule.

What is your subprocessor list?

Our subprocessor list is available upon request and in our DPA. We use AWS, Azure, and other leading cloud providers.

How do you handle security incidents?

We have a formal incident response plan with notification timelines: critical issues within 24 hours, others within 72 hours.

Is the platform available on government clouds?

Yes, we support deployment on AWS GovCloud, Azure Government, and other authorized government cloud environments.

Do you offer SSO and MFA?

Yes, SSO (SAML/OIDC) and MFA are included in all enterprise plans and can be enforced organization-wide.

Have security questions?

Our security team is available to address your specific requirements
