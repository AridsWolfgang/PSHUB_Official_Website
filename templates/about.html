Our Story

From Insight to Operating System

PsHUB was founded on a simple observation: Prosperity initiatives fail not from lack of effort, but from lack of systemic coordination.

Across Africa and the Global South, development has long been hindered by fragmented, siloed investments that fail to institutionalize impact at the sub-national level. To bridge this gap, the Prosperity Systems Hub (PsHUB) was established as the consulting, technology, and execution arm of Prosperity360. PsHUB functions as a commercially governed enterprise that transforms development ambition into durable, digitally enabled operating systems.

By integrating five core business lines—including GovTech, systems consulting, and scalable SaaS platforms like SLP+ and DLX+—PsHUB moves beyond traditional advisory roles to build institutional capability. With a ten-year strategic vision to power 7,000 Prosperity Zones and digitilize 500 local governments by 2035, PsHUB aims to replace episodic, grant-only-based interventions with coordinated, market-anchored systems. Ultimately, PsHUB serves as the commercial backbone necessary to convert policy intent into measurable, compounding prosperity outcomes.
