FAQs

Common questions about our process

How long does the entire process take?

Typical end-to-end deployment takes 12-18 months, but we design for phased implementation so you can see value early. The diagnostic phase typically takes 2-3 months.

What level of institutional commitment is required?

We require executive sponsorship and dedicated working group participation. The level of commitment scales with implementation phase, with most intensive involvement during design and deployment.

How do you ensure institutional ownership after deployment?

We design governance frameworks that put institutions in the lead from day one. Our capacity building programs ensure your team has the skills to operate and optimize the system independently.

Can the process be adapted for existing systems?

Yes, our methodology is designed to integrate with and enhance existing systems. We don't replace what works ‚ÄĒ we connect and optimize it.
